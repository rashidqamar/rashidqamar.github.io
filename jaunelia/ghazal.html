Jaun Elia

1931 - 2002 | Karachi, Pakistan

One of the most prominent modern Pakistani poets, popular for his unconventional ways.

Ghazals Of Jaun Elia

umr guzregi imtihan mein kya

khamoshi kah rahi hai mein kya

naya ek rishta paida kyun karen hum

kitne aish se rahte honge kitne itraate honge

be-qarari si be-qarari hai

haalat-e-haal ke sabab haalat-e-haal hi gai

be-dili kya yunhi din guzar jaenge

Thik hai KHud ko hum badalte hain

apne sab yar kaam kar rahe hain

gahe gahe bas ab yahi ho kya

tumhaara hijr mana lun agar ijazat ho

sar hi ab phoDiye nadamat mein

ek hunar hai jo kar gaya hun main

abhi ek shor sa uTha hai kahin

ek hi muzhda subh lati hai

bahut dil ko kushada kar liya kya

sina dahak raha ho to kya chup rahe koi

baDa ehsan hum farma rahe hain

aadmi waqt par gaya hoga

zabt kar ke hansi ko bhul gaya

kis se izhaar-e-muddaa kije

tu bhi chup hai main bhi chup hun ye kaisi tanhai hai

iza-dahi ki dad jo pata raha hun main

aaKHiri bar aah kar li hai

hum to jaise wahan ke the hi nahin

apna KHaka lagta hun

kaam ki baat maine ki hi nahin

aap apna ghubar the hum to

ji hi ji mein wo jal rahi hogi

dil jo hai aag laga dun usko

chalo baad-e-bahaari ja rahi hai

sharmindagi hai hum ko bahut hamm mile tumhein

uske pahlu se lag ke chalte hain

ghar se hum ghar talak gae honge

dil ne wafa ke nam par kar-e-wafa nahin kiya

har dhaDkan haijaani thi har KHamoshi tufani thi

zindagi kya hai ek kahani hai

hum ji rahe hain koi bahana kiye baghair

dil ki taklif kam nahin karte

ab wo ghar ek virana tha bas virana zinda tha

ruh pyasi kahan se aati hai

tum haqiqat nahin ho hasrat ho

jo hua jaun wo hua bhi nahin

ab kisi se mera hisab nahin

wo jo tha wo kabhi mila hi nahin

aaj lab-e-guhar-fishan aapne wa nahin kiya

sare rishte tabah kar aaya

juz guman aur tha hi kya mera

tang aaghosh mein aabaad karunga tujhko

abhi farman aaya hai wahan se

aish-e-ummid hi se KHatra hai

ai subah main ab kahan raha hun

KHub hai shauq ka ye pahlu bhi

hai fasilen uTha raha mujh mein

kabhi jab muddaton ke baad us ka samna hoga

zaKHm-e-ummid bhar gaya kab ka

hai ajab haal ye zamane ka

hum aandhiyon ke ban mein kisi karwan ke the

hai bikharne ko ye mahfil-e-rang-o-bu tum kahan jaoge hum kahan jaenge

kisi se ahd-o-paiman kar na rahiyo

lamhe lamhe ki na-rasai hai

kaam mujhse koi hua hi nahin

hijr ki aankhon se aankhen to milate jaiye

na hua nasib qarar-e-jaan hawas-e-qarar bhi ab nahin

hum kahan aur tum kahan jaanan

abhi kabhi to bahut yaad aane lagte ho

ajab haalat hamari ho gai hai

zikr bhi us se kya bhala mera

hamare zaKHm-e-tamanna purane ho gae hain

jo zindagi bachi hai use mat gawaiye

aaj bhi tishnagi ki qismat mein

apni manzil ka rasta bhejo

ai ku-e-yar tere zamane guzar gaye

baat koi ummid ki mujhse nahi kahi gayi

ai wasl kuchh yahan na hua kuchh nahin hua
